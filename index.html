The Concept

What is ACS?

ACS is a private-format cyber lounge with separate PC rooms, VIP spaces, and a full-service restaurant — built for those who demand more than a standard gaming cafe.

Premium Setup

Private Rooms Only

No strangers. Your session, your space. Every room is booked exclusively for your group — complete privacy guaranteed.

Premium Setups

Top-tier hardware at every station — 240Hz monitors, mechanical keyboards, high-performance PCs — built for serious players.

In-Room Food & Drinks

Order from our full restaurant menu directly from your PC. Food and drinks delivered to your room without missing a second of play.
